Course overview

This self‑study course teaches you how to audit desktop and mobile websites against WCAG 2.2 AA. It’s practical, tool‑based, and portfolio‑oriented.

  • Two parts: Elementary and Advanced.
  • Free tools only (NVDA or VoiceOver, axe DevTools, WAVE, Colour Contrast Analyser).
  • Downloadable templates and a worked examples pack.
  • Progress tracking saved to your browser.
